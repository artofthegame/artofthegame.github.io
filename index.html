The Art of the Game

UC Berkeley Spring 2018 Decal

This Art of the Game is for anyone and everyone with an interest and love for strategy games. This course is designed for people with an ambition to build their game skills, regardless of prior experience. Students who are new to a game have the opportunity to learn the rules and basics of a game. Students with prior experience in a particular game have the opportunity to refine their skills.
