Readings

The majority of the work for this class will be the readings

Hearts

The rules for Hearts are fairly straightforward but I would recommend reading up on the strategy and playing with the simulator before coming to class.

Spades / Tarneeb

The rules for Tarneeb are similar to that of Hearts on the outside. However, the betting element in Tarneeb adds another layer to the game. Definitely read up on the rules but I would put more emphasis on the betting aspects of the game.
